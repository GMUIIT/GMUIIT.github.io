Workshops

This is for the webdevs to fill out

In this KiCAD workshop, IIT will demonstrate why KiCAD is such an important tool when designing circuits or other electrical projects. We will teach members how to make a new KiCAD project, how to find some components like capacitors, resistors, and voltage sources and how to manipulate them in the KiCAD software such as rotating the pieces and adding wires between components. We will then explain how to export a project and how to add stuff from digikey. Finally, members will put all their new knowledge together and create a circuit of their own!
