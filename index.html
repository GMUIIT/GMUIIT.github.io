Welcome to the The Inventors and Innovations Team!

The mission of the Inventors and Innovations Team (IIT) is to connect like-minded students to express their interests in engineering and technology, to actively build and bring their ideas to reality. The focus is to give students the chance to develop their technical skills through hands-on construction of devices. IIT wishes to create an environment where all student ideas are accepted.
