Gameboy Project

The gameboy project is close to the hearts of many team members. To celebrate this device, we've decided to create our own gameboy from the ground up. This project is not for the faint of heart but highly rewarding. Starting with the Z80 microprocessor, SRAM, VRAM, and about 10 breadboards, we plan to bring a gameboy capable of loading and playing gameboy games to life this semester .
